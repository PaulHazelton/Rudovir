## Rules

The player bets any amount, chooses a number between 1 and 6. The dealer than rolls 3d6.

If one die matches the player's number, they get their money back plus an equal amount.

if two dice match the player's number, they get their money back plus double the amount.

If three dice match the player's number, they get their money back plus triple the amount.
