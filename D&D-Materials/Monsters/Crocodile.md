# Actions
- **_Bite_**. _Melee Weapon Attack_: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. _Hit_: 7 (1d10 + 2) piercing damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 12). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the crocodile can't bite another target.
