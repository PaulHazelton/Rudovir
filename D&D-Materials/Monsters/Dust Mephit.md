# Traits
- _**Death Burst.**_ When the mephit dies, it explodes in a burst of dust. Each creature within 5 feet of it must then succeed on a DC 10 Constitution saving throw or be [blinded](https://www.dndbeyond.com/compendium/rules/basic-rules/appendix-a-conditions#Blinded) for 1 minute. A [blinded](https://www.dndbeyond.com/compendium/rules/basic-rules/appendix-a-conditions#Blinded) creature can repeat the saving throw on each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

- _**Innate Spellcasting (1/Day).**_ The mephit can innately cast [sleep](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2254-sleep), requiring no material components. Its innate spellcasting ability is Charisma.
# Actions
- _**Claws.** Melee Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. _Hit:_ 4 (1d4 + 2) slashing damage.

- _**Blinding Breath (Recharge 6).**_ The mephit exhales a 15- foot cone of blinding dust. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity saving throw or be [blinded](https://www.dndbeyond.com/compendium/rules/basic-rules/appendix-a-conditions#Blinded) for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
