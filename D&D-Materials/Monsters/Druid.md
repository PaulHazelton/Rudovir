# Traits
- Spellcasting. The druid is a 4th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 12, +4 to hit with spell attacks). It has the following druid spells prepared:
	- Cantrips (at will): _[druidcraft](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/sorts.php?vo=druidcraft), [produce flame](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/sorts.php?vo=produce-flame), [shillelagh](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/sorts.php?vo=shillelagh)_  
	- 1st level (4 slots): _[entangle](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/sorts.php?vo=entangle), [longstrider](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/sorts.php?vo=longstrider), [speak with animals](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/sorts.php?vo=speak-with-animals), [cure wounds](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Cure%20Wounds#content)
	- 2nd level (3 slots): _[animal messenger](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/sorts.php?vo=animal-messenger), [barkskin](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/sorts.php?vo=barkskin)_
# Actions
- **_Quarterstaff_**. _Melee Weapon Attack_: +2 to hit (+4 to hit with _[shillelagh](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/sorts.php?vo=shillelagh)_), reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit_: 3 (1d6) bludgeoning damage, 4 (1d8) bludgeoning damage if wielded with two hands, or 6 (1d8 + 2) bludgeoning damage with _[shillelagh](https://www.aidedd.org/dnd/sorts.php?vo=shillelagh)_.
