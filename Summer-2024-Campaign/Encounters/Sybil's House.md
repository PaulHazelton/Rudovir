[[Sybil Faust]]'s house, which is a bit south of the rest of [[Dragonmouth]], about a 20 to 30 minute walk from town. Sybil and her child live hear.

> [!quote] Description
> You see a quaint little cottage nestled on top of a small hill in the forest. It looks old and run down, as if poorly maintained, but still lived in. There are some children's toys scattered around the front yard.

# Encounter Stuff

- 2 [[Shadow]]s are hiding near her house
- It is sunny out, but they are hiding in the shade casted by the house.
- [[Sybil Faust|Sybil]] will come out and help when she hears a commotion
	- "Stay inside, Isaac!"

- She is under a nerfed [Geas Spell](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Geas#content) that prevents her from speaking about the beast without taking 1d12 psychic damage.

- She will mention the beast on a DC 15 intimidation check.

- She will mention the children's books, and her son Isaac may bring out a children's book about [[The Bedlam Beldam]]
