*[[Sprinkles Tumtil]] vs. [[Sybil Faust]]*

Sprinkles, being the de facto ruler of the town, has set up a trial to accuse [[Sybil Faust|Sybil]] of kidnapping the children. He is loudly speaking and proclaiming, running the trial.

# The Scene

Sprinkles is at a podium addressing the crowd.

Sybil's hands are bound and she is tied to a wooden railing next to a noose.

Mind the Goblin sits at the witness stand.

A crowd of the townspeople have gathered at the trial. They are listening intently.

Notable townspeople:
- [[Goose]], father of Regan
	- Goose wants to know information about his lost daughter.
- Joyce Piebaker, mother of Charley and Penelope.
- Franklin and Lenny Smith, fathers of Lucy
- Jackpot Janice, owner of [[The Greedy Dragon]], which Sybil frequents.
- Adrian Moody.
	- Works at the Paper Mill, has an inkling about the bunker where he'd hide.
# Dialogue

> Sprinkles: Sybil Faust, you are hereby accused of abducting 7 (or 8?) children - including your very own child, and murdering them in cold blood.
> The evidence for such heinous crimes includes:
> 1. a map, found in possetion by this here Goblin, describing where to take the children where to recieve payment. The map points to the house of the accused for payment, and to a grim site in the woods.
> 2. We recovered the bones of children at that site.
> 3. And finally, first hand testimony by our star witness.
> Sir Mind, can you confirm?

> Mind: Yeah that lady definitely paid us to kidnap those kids. I'd hang her for sure.

> Sprinkles: What does the accused have to say for herself?

> Sybil: I was forced to! By the Beldam! It even took my own son!

> Sprinkles: Hah! The beast from the children's books we print!

> Sybil: It spoke to me! It tried to take Isaac!

> Sprinkles: Nonsense! If it does exist I bet you summoned it!

> Crowd: Hang her already!
