## Reward

- The bush will offer 4 green and 4 red of [[Harry's Berries]] as a reward.
- The bush has 4 extra berries available to be stolen (7 total)
## The Cliff and Cave:

- It's a rock formation, like 15 feet high.
- You can't see the Bear from the ground.
- (DC 13 Climb) to get on top of the cliff.
- A [[Brown Bear]] will be in a small bear cave.
- When they first see him he's puking violently.

The [[Brown Bear]]
- -1 DEX and -1 STR

- A piece of a bush with 3 [[Harry's Berries]] (stolen)
	- If brought back to Harry, he let's the players have them.
