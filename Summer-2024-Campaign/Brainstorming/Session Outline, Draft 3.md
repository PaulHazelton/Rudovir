# Session 1

## Plot
- Children are being kidnapped.
- Party is hired to save them, and figure out who is responsible.
- Quick adventure to fight [[Dragonmouth Band Of Goblins|goblins]] and save the children.
- [[Dragonmouth Band Of Goblins|Goblins]] reveal that [[Sybil Faust]] hired them.
- Return children, collect reward.
- Confront [[Sybil Faust|Sybil]]
- She is in debt to [[Sprinkles Tumtil]], and she thinks he might know more information.
- Head off to find [[Sprinkles Tumtil]].
## Activities

- Storytelling
	- There should be some nice exposition to the world, the town, and for the characters and their backstory.
- Combat (with goblins)
- Rewards
	- Either the [[Dragonmouth Band Of Goblins|goblin]] fight or the [[Sybil Faust|Sybil]] encounter should leave the players with rewards.
- Interrogation
	- The [[Dragonmouth Band Of Goblins|goblins]] will reveal [[Sybil Faust]]
- Investigation
	- If they kill all the [[Dragonmouth Band Of Goblins|goblins]], there should be evidence that points to [[Sybil Faust]]
