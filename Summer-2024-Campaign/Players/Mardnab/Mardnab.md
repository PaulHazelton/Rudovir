# Backstory

>Mardnab grew up in [[The Bog]] and has only ever known chaotic and violent goblin society in a dreary marsh setting. She is capable with her fists as a means to survive. [[Palovo Missionary Cleric|A cleric]] came to The Bog proselytizing [[The Pantheon#Palovo the God of Peace|Palovo the God of Peace]] which deeply moved her. She latched onto their words about communing with nature and living in peace. She has left the Bog to pursue her curiosity of the world outside with a specific interest in understanding Palovo through nature.
