[[Commoner]]

Owner of [[The Silly Goose Tavern]]. A nice old man who's nice to [[Ebony Lyrathal]] and provides her with lodging in exchange for her performances.

His **daughter Regan** disappeared 20 years ago when she was a child.

His daughter called him a "silly goose" and he named the tavern to honor her. His real name is **Bruce Aleman**.
