[[Joro]] / [[Palos]]

"The Kingdom in the East." Eckeron is the capitol city that unites [[Joro]] and [[Palos]].
Eckeron is a massive fortified city split by the [[Valon Strait]] (narrow river) and united by the [[Continental Crossing]] (the bridge).

**Queen:** [[Sylvin Lightfoot]] "The Queen of the East".
